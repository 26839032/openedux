Signals / Self

Still wandering.
A map that is never finished.

Some thoughts drift through quietly. Others linger for years. This is not a tracking system or a productivity exercise. It is an inner observatory — a place where signals from the self can surface, shift, and slowly become visible over time.

We move through life without seeing the full map.

Sometimes the signal is weak.

Sometimes wandering is the path itself.
