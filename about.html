About

Exploring practical AI systems for modern work and learning.

I'm a mid-career manager with a technical background working in a global enterprise environment. Over the years, I've become increasingly interested in how AI can reduce cognitive load, improve decision-making, and create more sustainable ways of working and learning.

OpenEdux is a long-term exploration of AI-assisted workflows, knowledge systems, productivity design, and modern learning methods — built from real-world work pressure, experimentation, and curiosity.

This site is where I document thoughts, systems, experiments, and small improvements that help make work and life a little more manageable in the age of AI.
